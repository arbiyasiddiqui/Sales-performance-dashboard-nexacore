# 📊 Sales Performance Dashboard – NexaCore Digital Solutions

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing the sales performance of NexaCore Digital Solutions using Excel / Google Sheets. The goal of the project was to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights that help understand revenue trends, customer behavior, and service performance across different cities and departments.

The dashboard provides a clear view of company sales performance and helps management identify opportunities for improving revenue and profitability.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
NexaCore Digital Solutions offers multiple digital services to different types of customers. However, the company faced several challenges:

- Inconsistent sales performance across different months
- Heavy dependency on repeat customers
- Limited visibility into which services generate the most revenue
- Difficulty identifying the most profitable customer segments and cities

To solve these issues, a sales analysis dashboard was developed to track key performance indicators and visualize important sales metrics.

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel / Google Sheets
- Data Analysis
- Data Visualization

---

## 📂 Dataset Description
The dataset contains sales transaction data for NexaCore Digital Solutions. Each record includes information related to sales performance such as:

- Date of sale
- Customer name and customer type
- Service provided
- Department responsible for the service
- Customer city
- Sales amount
- Profit margin percentage
- Margin amount
- Sale type (New or Repeat)
- Customer source
- Package type

This data helps analyze both revenue generation and business profitability.

---

## 📊 Key KPIs
The following key performance indicators were created to measure business performance:

- **Total Sales Revenue**
- **Total Profit (Margin Amount)**
- **Average Profit Margin**
- **Total Customers**
- **Repeat Sales Contribution**

These KPIs provide a quick overview of the company's overall performance.

---

## 📈 Dashboard Analysis
The dashboard includes several visualizations to analyze sales performance:

### Sales Trend Analysis
Shows how sales change over time to identify growth trends and seasonal patterns.

### Service-wise Revenue Analysis
Displays which services generate the highest revenue.

### City-wise Sales Distribution
Highlights cities contributing the most to company sales.

### Customer Type Analysis
Compares revenue generated from companies versus individual customers.

### Profit Margin Analysis
Evaluates profitability across different services and packages.

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
Some important insights identified from the analysis include:

- Certain services contribute significantly more revenue than others.
- Sales performance varies across cities.
- Repeat customers contribute a large portion of company revenue.
- Profit margins differ across services and packages.

These insights help management understand business performance more clearly.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
Based on the analysis, the following recommendations were suggested:

- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing services
- Increase efforts to attract new customers
- Expand business operations in high-performing cities
- Promote services with higher profit margins

These actions can help improve company revenue and overall profitability.
