# Sales Performance Dashboard – NexaCore Digital Solutions

## 1. Project Overview
The Sales Performance Dashboard project focuses on analyzing the sales data of NexaCore Digital Solutions to understand revenue trends, customer behavior, and service performance. The objective of this project was to convert raw sales data into meaningful insights that can help management make better business decisions. By analyzing the sales dataset, the dashboard provides a clear view of company performance across different services, cities, departments, and customer segments.

## 2. Business Problem
NexaCore Digital Solutions offers multiple services such as marketing and digital solutions to different types of customers. However, the company faced challenges in understanding which services were generating the highest revenue, which cities were contributing the most to sales, and which customer segments were most profitable. Sales performance was also inconsistent across different months, and management needed a better way to track and monitor sales performance.

Without a centralized dashboard, it was difficult for decision-makers to quickly understand sales trends, profit margins, and customer behavior. Therefore, the company needed a data-driven solution that could visually represent key sales metrics and help identify growth opportunities.

## 3. Objective of the Project
The main objectives of this project were:

- Analyze overall sales performance of the company
- Identify high-performing services and departments
- Understand customer behavior and customer types
- Evaluate sales distribution across different cities
- Analyze profit margins and revenue contribution
- Create an interactive dashboard for better decision-making

## 4. Dataset Description
The dataset used in this project contains sales transaction records for NexaCore Digital Solutions. Each record represents a sales entry with multiple attributes that help analyze business performance.

Key columns included in the dataset:

- Date – Date when the sale occurred
- Month – Month of the transaction
- Year – Year of the sale
- Customer Name – Name of the customer
- Customer Type – Individual or Company
- Service – Type of service sold
- Department – Department responsible for the service
- City – Location of the customer
- Sales Amount – Total revenue generated from the sale
- Margin % – Profit margin percentage
- Margin Amount – Profit generated from the sale
- Sale Type – New sale or repeat sale
- Customer Source – Source through which the customer came
- Package Type – Type of service package (Monthly / Annual)

This dataset enables analysis of both revenue and profitability across different dimensions.

## 5. Data Preparation
Before building the dashboard, the dataset was cleaned and prepared in Excel / Google Sheets. The following steps were performed:

- Removed extra spaces and formatting issues in numerical columns
- Converted sales amount and margin values into numeric format
- Standardized column names and data types
- Verified date format for time-based analysis
- Checked for missing or inconsistent values

Data preparation ensured that the dataset was accurate and ready for analysis.

## 6. Key KPIs Created
Several key performance indicators (KPIs) were created to evaluate company performance:

- Total Sales Revenue – Overall revenue generated
- Total Profit (Margin Amount) – Total profit from all sales
- Average Profit Margin – Average profitability across services
- Total Customers – Number of customers served
- Repeat Sales Percentage – Contribution from repeat customers

These KPIs provide a quick summary of overall business performance.

## 7. Dashboard Design
The Sales Performance Dashboard was designed to present important business metrics through clear and interactive visualizations.

The dashboard includes:

### Sales Trend Analysis
A time-based chart showing how sales change across months and years. This helps identify seasonal patterns and sales growth trends.

### Service-wise Revenue Analysis
A chart showing revenue generated by different services offered by the company. This helps identify the most profitable services.

### City-wise Sales Distribution
A visualization showing how sales are distributed across different cities. This helps understand regional performance.

### Customer Type Analysis
Analysis of sales generated from different customer types such as companies and individual clients.

### Profit Margin Analysis
A chart evaluating profit margins across services to identify which services provide the highest profitability.

These visualizations help decision-makers quickly understand the performance of the business.

## 8. Key Insights
The analysis revealed several valuable insights:

- Some services contributed significantly more revenue than others.
- Certain cities generated higher sales compared to others.
- Repeat customers played an important role in maintaining sales stability.
- Profit margins varied across different services and packages.
- Some services generated high revenue but lower profit margins.

These insights help the company better understand which areas drive business growth.

## 9. Business Recommendations
Based on the analysis, the following recommendations were suggested:

- Focus marketing efforts on high-performing services.
- Expand operations in cities with strong sales performance.
- Improve strategies to convert new customers into repeat customers.
- Review pricing strategies for services with lower profit margins.
- Promote high-margin services to increase overall profitability.

Implementing these recommendations can help NexaCore Digital Solutions improve revenue growth and profitability.

## 10. Conclusion
This project demonstrates how data analysis and visualization can transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights. By creating an interactive Sales Performance Dashboard, the company can monitor its performance, track key metrics, and make data-driven decisions. The dashboard provides a clear overview of sales trends, customer behavior, and service profitability, helping management focus on strategies that drive business growth.
